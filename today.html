About the Museum

Welcome to The Indian Museum, Kolkata â€“ the oldest and one of the largest museums in India. Established in 1814, this historic institution houses an extensive collection of artifacts spanning thousands of years of Indian history and culture. With exhibits ranging from ancient sculptures and artifacts to rare coins, fossils, and precious gems, the museum offers a fascinating journey through India's rich heritage.

Located in the heart of Kolkata, the museum's neoclassical architecture itself is a testament to the grandeur of India's colonial past. It is renowned not only for its impressive collection but also for its role in the advancement of archaeological and anthropological studies in India.

Whether you're a history enthusiast, a student, or a curious traveler, The Indian Museum promises an enriching experience that brings the ancient and modern worlds together.
